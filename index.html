Cancer Screening

With the use of modern technology

We offer screening services of various cancers including breast, cervical as well as prostrate. This enables our clients receive the necessary timely assistance needed.

Hemodialysis

We have a 6-bed capacity renal unit that is well equipped with modern technology and equipment

experienced renal nurses and a nephrologist thus ensuring we provide you with the care you deserve.

Mental Health, Psychotherapy, Counselling and Wellness services

We are the only medical facility in the region offering psychological care and support

We specialize in mental health education, psychoanalysis and psychological therapy that aids in early mitigation of mental problems.

Preventive Services

We provide health education to our clients and communities on healthy lifestyle and health promotive behaviors

We offer medical check-up services, early screening for chronic diseases, community outreaches, as well as immunizations against communicable diseases.

Maternity & Reproductive Health Services

We provide comprehensive maternity/ reproductive health services that include:-

Modern family planning services
Preconception care
Antenatal care
Yoga and Lamaze for pregnant mothers
Delivery and newborn care services
Postnatal services
Post-abortal care
Child welfare clinic and immunization services
Counselling
Obstetric Ultrasound services
